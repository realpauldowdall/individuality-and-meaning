The Ground

we are born needy,

we are taught stories,

we mistake stories for reality,

life and loss strip things back,

what remains is a simple life,

the company of friends,

and the comfort we give and receive,

we die.

notice,

inspect,

do not confuse the record with the thing,

do not confuse the category with the person,

do not confuse managed appearance with reality,

and do not forget that, for everyone,

life ends.

Companionship and comfort,

well given,

are well remembered.
